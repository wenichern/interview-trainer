Key Reminders:

  • STAR Method: Situation, Task, Action, Result
  • Headline First: Answer the question directly, then provide details
  • 60-90 Seconds: Keep your answers concise and focused
  • Measurable Results: Always include numbers and metrics
  • Confident Language: Replace "I guess" with "I believe"
  • Own Your Work: Say "I" not "we" when describing your contributions
