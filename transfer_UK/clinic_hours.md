## 📊 Suggested Hour Distribution Strategy

```
Total Target: 100–300 Hours
├── 🏆 Tier 1 Clinical Roles (ED, ICU Nursing Units)     → 60–120 hrs
├── 🥈 Tier 2 Specialty Clinics (Oncology, GI, Peds ED)  → 40–80 hrs
└── 🥉 Tier 3 Soft Skills (Compassionate Companions)     → 20–40 hrs
```

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** Choose **2–3 roles max** and commit consistently.  
> Admissions committees value **depth, reflection, and consistency** over quantity.
